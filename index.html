About Ardhah Project

Senior project, Sep 29, 2024

Ardhah is a platform specialized in managing and showcasing advertising billboards. It allows users to easily browse available billboard locations, view detailed information about each space, and make direct booking requests. The platform was designed to streamline the advertising process, helping companies and individuals find the perfect advertising spots efficiently.
